CUBRIC has gathered neuroimaging data (“MEG UK Data”) as a result of the use of MEG and MRI Systems at eight Universities: Aston, Cambridge, Cardiff, Glasgow, Nottingham, Oxford, UCL and York (“the UK MEG Partnership”). CUBRIC is willing and able to make selected data available to researchers at other Universities (“the Researcher”) for research purposes, subject to the Researcher being aware of and complying with specific obligations (“MEG UK Obligations”) relating to the use of the MEG UK Data.

The following is a non-exhaustive summary of the main MEG UK Obligations:

  • the Researcher shall not use the Requested Data provided for any commercial purpose or for commercially sponsored research;
  • the Researcher shall use the Requested Data provided for the purposes of the Project only;
  • the Researcher will not transfer, distribute, release or disclose the Requested Data to any other organisation or institution, or to any individuals who are not members of the Research Team;
  • the Researcher shall have appropriate measures in place to ensure the confidentiality of the MEG UK Data in the Researcher’s custody and shall not attempt to obtain information identifying the individuals who have provided the MEG UK Data;
  • it is imperative that the Researcher does not (and procures that the Research Team does not) try to identify any individuals from the Requested Data provided.

This document outlines the MEG UK Obligations which the Researcher must respect when using the MEG UK Data. It covers the arrangements which are needed to ensure effective and efficient research governance and aims to establish a clear understanding of the roles and responsibilities of the Researcher.

The Researcher has submitted a request to CUBRIC for neuroimaging data as more fully described in Schedule 1 (“the Requested Data”) which forms part of the MEG UK Data for use by the Researcher and the Researcher’s team of researchers (“the Research Team”) to carry out the project detailed in Schedule 1 (“the Project”).

The Researcher shall use the Requested Data for the purposes of the Project only. The Researcher shall not use the Requested Data for any commercial purpose or for commercially sponsored research without seeking written consent from CUBRIC.

a. expiry of the Term;

b. at CUBRIC’s request.
